Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching you total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes adove the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
